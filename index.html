Michigan Justice Fund

Tracking rapid response resources and ICE activity across Michigan to protect and support our communities.

Communities need real-time information. When ICE activity is reported or when someone needs immediate support, every minute matters. Delays mean missed opportunities to help, to warn, to protect.

This map provides a fast, reliable way to track service providers and ICE sightings across Michigan. It's built for organizers, advocates, and community members who need accurate information right now—without waiting on complex systems or outdated databases.
